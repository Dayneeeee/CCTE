Who We Are

Building Competent
Technology Professionals

The College of Computing and Technology Engineering at Lipa City Colleges provides students with strong foundations in computing, engineering technology, innovation, and practical problem-solving skills for today's digital economy.

We promote hands-on learning, collaborative research, technical skill-building, and global exposure through academic programs and international activities like the Taiwan Science Camp.

Mission

Develop competent, responsible, and innovative students equipped with technical knowledge, professional values, and industry-ready skills.

Vision

Become a recognized center of excellence in computing, technology, engineering, and global academic collaboration.

Our Foundation

Core Values

Innovation

We encourage creative thinking, novel solutions, and technology-driven approaches to real-world challenges.

Integrity

We uphold honesty, transparency, and ethical conduct in all academic and professional endeavors.

Collaboration

We build strong teamwork, partnerships, and a supportive academic community for collective growth.

Excellence

We strive for the highest standards in education, research output, and professional performance.

Global Mindset

We embrace international perspectives and prepare students for a connected, competitive world.

Service

We are committed to using technology as a force for positive impact in our community and society.
