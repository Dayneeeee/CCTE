World-Class Facilities

Modern, purpose-built spaces designed for hands-on learning, collaboration, and technological innovation.

Computer Laboratory

Fully equipped for programming, web development, and software training with high-performance hardware and licensed software.

Electronics Laboratory

Hands-on stations for circuit design, embedded systems, microcontroller programming, and automation project development.

Research Area

A dedicated space for capstone development, technical documentation, peer review, and thorough project testing.

Innovation Hub

A creative collaboration space where student teams prototype, iterate, and develop technology-driven solutions.

Computing

Linux Laboratory

High-performance workstations, development servers, and cloud environments for coding, software development, and IT training. Equipped with licensed software tools for programming, design, and systems analysis.

Engineering

Smart Laboratory

Soldering stations, oscilloscopes, power supplies, circuit boards, and components for hands-on electronics and embedded systems learning. Ideal for microcontroller programming and automation projects.

Research

Work Shop

Dedicated spaces for academic research projects, data analysis, technical writing, and collaborative student-faculty research initiatives. Supports capstone development, peer review, and project documentation.

Innovation

Innovation Hub

Prototyping and design space with 3D printers, cutting tools, design software, and maker equipment. A creative collaboration space where student teams prototype, iterate, and build technology-driven solutions.
